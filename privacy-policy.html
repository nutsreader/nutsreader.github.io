Privacy Policy for iStreamZ

Last Updated: August 10, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to iStreamZ. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about you when you use our mobile application. By using iStreamZ, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your display name, email address, and password. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email, and profile picture as provided by Google.
2. User-Generated Content: We collect information you provide, such as your list of followed ('My List') and reserved shows, and your watched history.
3. Device Information: We automatically collect your Advertising ID for ad personalization.
4. Third-Party Data: Our app displays information (titles, descriptions, images) from The Movie Database (TMDB) API but we do not send any of your personal data to TMDB.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
- Personalize your experience.
- Authenticate your account and prevent fraud.
- Serve personalized advertisements through Google AdMob.
- Communicate with you about your account or our services.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with third-party service providers for:
- Cloud Hosting: Your data is stored using Google Firebase services (Firestore, Firebase Authentication).
- Advertising: We share your device's Advertising ID with Google AdMob to serve personalized ads.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. Your password is encrypted and we do not have access to it.
